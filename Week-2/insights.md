Key Insights
1. Regional Performance
The Westregion generated the highest sales among all regions, indicating strong customer demand.
2. Category Analysis
 The Technology category recorded the highest sales and profit, making it the best-performing product category.
3. Top Customers
A small number of customers contributed a significant portion of the total sales, highlighting the importance of retaining high-value customers.
4. Monthly Sales Trend
Sales varied across different months, with noticeable peaks during the year-end period, suggesting seasonal buying patterns.
5. Profitability
Some orders generated **negative profit**, indicating losses that may have resulted from high discounts or low-margin products.
6. Top-Selling Products**
A few products consistently achieved the highest sales, making them key contributors to overall business revenue.
7. Data Quality
The dataset contained no major missing values. Duplicate **Order IDs** were observed, but this is expected because a single order can include multiple products rather than indicating duplicate records.
